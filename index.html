Conflict Resolution Cheatsheet

Understanding Conflict

Conflict is a natural part of life and can arise from differences in interests, values, or goals.

Conflict Resolution Techniques

  • Active Listening
  • Empathy
  • Negotiation
  • Mediation
  • Collaboration

Steps to Resolve Conflict

  1. Identify the source of conflict.
  2. Understand the perspectives of all parties involved.
  3. Discuss possible solutions.
  4. Agree on a solution.
  5. Follow up to ensure the resolution is working.
